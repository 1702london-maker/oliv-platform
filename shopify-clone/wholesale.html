The Programme

Built for Professionals

Our wholesale programme is designed for salons, stylists, retailers and professionals who want access to high-quality BiziLux products at competitive pricing.

We work with a selective network of wholesale partners who share our commitment to quality and client experience. Every application is reviewed personally by our team to ensure the right fit.

Once approved, you gain direct access to our full BiziLux product range, dedicated support and a simple ordering system designed to save you time.
