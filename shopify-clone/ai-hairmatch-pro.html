Expert Styling Advice

Styling Advice

Your oval face shape means you can wear almost any style. We recommend soft, face-framing layers to enhance your high cheekbones. Avoid extremely blunt cuts that hide your natural cheekbone structure.

Maintenance Advice

Human hair extensions require deep conditioning every 2 weeks. Use sulphate-free shampoo and silk pillowcases to preserve longevity. Avoid high heat without a heat protectant spray. Expected lifespan: 12–18 months with proper care.

Colour Advice

Your warm golden undertones are best complemented by chocolate browns, caramel highlights, and honey balayage. Avoid cool ash tones which can make warm skin appear sallow.

Installation Advice

We recommend a professional installation for lace frontals. Our Berlin salon team offers expert installation, customisation, and colour matching services.
