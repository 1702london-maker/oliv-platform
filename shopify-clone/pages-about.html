Our Story

From one chair
to a movement.

Olivhairsupply was built from real experience, not theory.

After years of working hands-on as hair extension specialists, selling and installing extensions for a wide range of clients, we saw the same problem again and again: inconsistent hair quality, unreliable tools, and products that simply did not meet professional standards.

Instead of accepting this as the norm, we chose to improve it.

Through continuous customer feedback and daily practical experience, we refined our understanding of what truly defines high quality hair — from texture and longevity to comfort and performance. This led to the development and curation of products that meet the expectations of both professionals and clients.

But our journey did not stop at hair.

As specialists ourselves, we experienced firsthand the difficulty of finding tools and appliances that were durable, efficient, and made for real salon use. Many products on the market failed to deliver long-term performance.

This is why we introduced BiziLuxe, a premium line of professional tools, accessories, and hair solutions, carefully researched and sourced to meet the demands of modern hairstylists. Every product is selected with a focus on durability, precision, and ease of use.

Today, OlivHairSupply serves as a one-stop destination for high quality hair, tools, and accessories built on expertise, shaped by real experience, and driven by the goal to provide better solutions for both professionals and everyday clients.

We don't just sell products.
We offer solutions we trust, use, and stand behind.
