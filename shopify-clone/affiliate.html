A Few Things to Know

We believe in being upfront. Here is what the programme does not include.

No guaranteed income. The programme is performance-based. Your earnings reflect the effort and quality of your promotion.

No automatic approval. Every application is reviewed by our team. We partner selectively to protect the standard of our brand.

No misuse of brand assets. All OlivHairSupply and BiziLux branding must be used in accordance with our affiliate guidelines.

No spam or unethical promotion. We expect our affiliates to promote authentically and in line with our brand values at all times.
